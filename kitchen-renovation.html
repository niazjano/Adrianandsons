Kitchen Modern

Project Overview

This comprehensive kitchen renovation transformed a dated, inefficient kitchen into a modern, functional space that serves as the heart of the home. The project focused on creating an open-plan layout with premium appliances, smart storage solutions, and contemporary design elements that enhance both functionality and aesthetics.

What We Delivered

  • Complete Redesign: Full kitchen layout redesign for optimal workflow
  • Modern Cabinetry: Custom kitchen units with soft-close mechanisms
  • Premium Appliances: Integrated fridge, dishwasher, and induction hob
  • Smart Features: LED lighting, underfloor heating, and smart controls
  • Premium Finishes: Quartz worktops, porcelain tiles, and designer fixtures
